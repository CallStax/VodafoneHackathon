The Għajnu.na platform is an online ecosystem where voluntary NGOs working in the social justice space help connect beneficiaries with benefactors.

In need of help?

Your request will go to one of our NGOs who will reach out to you to assess your case and determine how best to help you.

We appreciate that asking for help is not easy, which is why your privacy is of utmost importance to us and we go to great lengths to safeguard it.

At no point will you be asked to divulge any personal or sensitive information through the Application (all we need is your public key). If in doubt, reach out to your nearest NGO center where you can speak to a representative who can guide you through the process. We recommend you utilise a separate and unused account to get started.

We are always on the lookout to grow our network of NGOs and Benefactors. If you think you can contribute, please sign up by following the links below.
